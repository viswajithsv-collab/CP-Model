📊 AVAILABLE SIGNALS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

💓 LVP        - Left Ventricular Pressure (pink, 0-140 mmHg)
               Shows the pressure in the left ventricle during
               systole and diastole

💙 RVP        - Right Ventricular Pressure (cyan, 0-30 mmHg)
               Shows the pressure in the right ventricle

📦 LVV        - Left Ventricular Volume (pink, 50-150 ml)
               Shows how the LV fills and empties

📦 RVV        - Right Ventricular Volume (cyan, 50-150 ml)
               Shows how the RV fills and empties

🌊 Flows      - All 4 Valve Flows (multi-color)
               Aortic, Mitral, Pulmonary, Tricuspid
               Shows blood flow through each valve

🔄 PV Loops   - Pressure-Volume Loops (side-by-side)
               Classic cardiac function visualization
               Shows LV and RV work

🩸 Arterial   - Aortic & Pulmonary Pressures (orange & cyan)
               Systemic and pulmonary arterial pressures

🎮 HOW TO USE - STEP BY STEP
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: Start Simulation
   • Press ▶️  PLAY button
   • Wait ~2 seconds for computation
   • Plot appears with LVP signal (default)

STEP 2: Explore Signals
   • Click 💙 RVP to see right ventricle
   • Click 📦 LVV to see volume changes
   • Click 🌊 Flows to see all 4 valves
   • Click 🔄 PV to see classic loops

STEP 3: Adjust Parameters
   LEFT COLUMN:
   • Change "LV Contractility" → See BP change
   • Turn on "PUMP ON" → Simulate VAD support
   • Increase "Heart Rate" → See faster cycles
   • Adjust "Window" → Change visible timeframe

STEP 4: Observe Changes
   • Watch plot update in real-time
   • Check metrics at bottom
   • Notice hemodynamic effects

STEP 5: Export Data
   • 📊 CSV for all time-series data
   • ⚙️  Params for your settings
   • 📄 Report for summary

🎓 EDUCATIONAL SCENARIOS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1️⃣  NORMAL HEART
   • Keep all defaults
   • Click through signals
   • Learn normal waveforms

2️⃣  HEART FAILURE
   • Reduce LV Contractility to 0.8
   • Watch:
     - BP drops
     - CO decreases
     - PV loop widens
     - EF falls

3️⃣  VAD SUPPORT
   • Set LV Contractility to 0.8 (failing heart)
   • Turn Pump ON
   • Set Speed to 5000 RPM
   • Watch:
     - BP recovers
     - CO increases
     - LV unloading

4️⃣  VALVE DISEASE
   • Add Aortic Stenosis (0.5)
   • Observe:
     - Higher LV pressure
     - Reduced aortic flow
     - Pressure gradient

5️⃣  VOLUME CHANGES
   • Reduce Blood Volume to 4000 ml
   • See:
     - Lower filling pressures
     - Reduced CO
     - Smaller volumes

📈 METRICS EXPLAINED
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Blood Pressure (Systolic/Diastolic)
   → Peak and trough aortic pressure
   → Normal: ~120/80 mmHg

MAP (Mean Arterial Pressure)
   → Average pressure in aorta
   → Normal: ~90 mmHg

Cardiac Output (CO)
   → Blood pumped per minute
   → Normal: ~5 L/min

Stroke Volume (SV)
   → Blood pumped per beat
   → Normal: ~70 ml

Ejection Fraction (EF)
   → Percentage of blood ejected
   → Normal: ~60%

Mean PAP (Pulmonary Artery Pressure)
   → Average pulmonary pressure
   → Normal: ~15 mmHg
