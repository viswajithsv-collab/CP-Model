📊 AVAILABLE SIGNALS (7 Options)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

💓 LVP - Left Ventricular Pressure
   What: Pressure in left ventricle during cardiac cycle
   Range: 0-140 mmHg
   Color: Pink/Red
   Use: See systole (high) and diastole (low)

💙 RVP - Right Ventricular Pressure
   What: Pressure in right ventricle
   Range: 0-30 mmHg
   Color: Cyan
   Use: Monitor right heart function

📦 LVV - Left Ventricular Volume
   What: Volume changes in left ventricle
   Range: 50-150 ml
   Color: Pink
   Use: See filling and ejection

📦 RVV - Right Ventricular Volume
   What: Volume changes in right ventricle
   Range: 50-150 ml
   Color: Light Cyan
   Use: Monitor right heart volumes

🌊 Flows - All 4 Valve Flows
   What: Blood flow through each valve
   Includes: Aortic, Mitral, Pulmonary, Tricuspid
   Color: Multi-color (different for each)
   Use: See valve function and timing

🔄 PV Loops - Pressure-Volume Loops
   What: Classic cardiac function visualization
   Shows: LV and RV loops side-by-side
   Color: Pink (LV), Cyan (RV)
   Use: Assess cardiac work and contractility

🩸 Arterial - Arterial Pressures
   What: Systemic and pulmonary artery pressures
   Shows: Aortic (orange), Pulmonary (cyan)
   Use: Monitor blood pressure
